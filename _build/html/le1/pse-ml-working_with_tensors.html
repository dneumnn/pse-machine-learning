What is a tensor?

What is a tensor?#

A PyTorch Tensor is basically the same as a numpy array: it is just a generic n-dimensional array to be used for arbitrary numeric computation.

\begin{align*} \begin{bmatrix} 1 \ \end{bmatrix} && \begin{bmatrix} 1 \ 2 \ 3 \ \end{bmatrix} && \begin{bmatrix} 1 & 2 & 3 \ 4 & 5 & 6 \ 7 & 8 & 9 \ \end{bmatrix} && \begin{bmatrix} \begin{bmatrix} a & b \ c & d \ \end{bmatrix} & \begin{bmatrix} e & f \ g & h \ \end{bmatrix} \end{bmatrix} \end{align*}
